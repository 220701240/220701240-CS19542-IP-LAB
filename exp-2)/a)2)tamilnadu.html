TAMILNADU DETAILS

Government:

Chief Minister: M.K. Stalin, leader of the Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (DMK) party.

Governor: R.N. Ravi (as of the latest update).

Geography and Climate:

Location: Tamil Nadu is located in the southern part of India. It is bordered by the states of Kerala to the west, Karnataka to the northwest, Andhra Pradesh to the north, and the Bay of Bengal to the east. It shares a maritime border with the country of Sri Lanka to the southeast..

Area: Approximately 130,058 square kilometers.

Capital:Chennai.

Climate: Tamil Nadu has a tropical climate, with hot summers, mild winters, and two monsoon seasons: the southwest monsoon (June to September) and the northeast monsoon (October to December).

Demographics:

Population: Approximately 72 million people.

Languages: Tamil (official and widely spoken), English (for official and educational purposes)..
