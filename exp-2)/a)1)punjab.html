PUNJAB DETAILS

Government:

Chief Minister: Bhagwant Mann, from the Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) (as of the latest update).

Governor: Banwarilal Purohit (as of the latest update).

Geography and Climate:

Location: Punjab is located in the northwestern part of India. It is bordered by the Indian states of Jammu and Kashmir to the north, Himachal Pradesh to the northeast, Haryana to the south and southeast, and Rajasthan to the southwest. The international border with Pakistan lies to the west.

Area: Approximately 50,362 square kilometers.

Capital: Chandigarh (which is also the capital of the neighboring state of Haryana).

Climate: Punjab has a continental climate, with hot summers, cold winters, and a monsoon season from July to September..

Demographics:

Population: Approximately 30 million people.

Languages: Punjabi (official), with Hindi and English also widely spoken.
